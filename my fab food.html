RASGULLA

The dessert is known as Rosogolla or Roshogolla in Bengali and Rasagola in Odia. Rasgulla is derived from the words ras ("juice") and gulla ("ball"). Other names for the dish include Roshgulla (Sylheti), Rasagulla, Rossogolla, Roshogolla, Rasagola, Rasagolla, and Rasbhari or Rasbari (Nepali).

Ingredients of rasgulla

  1. Nestle A+ Milk
  2. Powder Sugar
  3. Green Elaichi
  4. Lemon Juice
  5. Water

Recipie of Rasgulla

Step by step procedure

  1. To make rasgullas,add 2 cups of Nestle A+ Milk in a pan,let it boil.
  2. Add 1.5 tsp lemon juice and stir it continuously to let the Nestlé A+ Milk Nestlé A+ Curdle.
  3. Mix well, remove from heat, and strain the Nestlé A+ Curdled Nestlé A+ Milk using a muslin cloth to drain out the whey.
  4. Transfer the paneer into a bowl, rub and knead till it softens.
  5. Roll into smooth mediumsized balls without cracks.
  6. For the chashni, heat 2 cups of water, 2.5 tbsp sugar powder, and 1 green elaichi in a pan.
  7. Now, slowly drop the prepared rasgullas into the simmering liquid.
  8. cover the pan with a lid and cook for 10 min.
  9. Remove from heat, allow to cool and serve.
  10. Serve it in plate.Now Rasgulla is Ready.
